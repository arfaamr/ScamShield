Scam emails and fraudulent messages can look professional and convincing. We strive to be able to aid our clients and prevent fraud; however, one should remain educated and informed regarding their online messages.

Here some actions you can take if you feel an email or text is suspicious:

  • Do not open any attachments or links
  • Do not send or forward the message to another person
  • Reporting the message
  • Blocking the email/ phone number


The best protection against scams is to stay informed and ask questions. More information about how to deal with scam messages can be found at The Government of Canada Website
